Openirekia-ios

Mobile client for the Basque Government's oGov platform

Welcome to OpenIrekia-iOS

As part of the Open Irekia project, the Basque Government has released under the EUPL bits of free software. This is an unofficial mirror of the software releases for the iOS mobile client, which works on iOS and iPad. See the github repo. Or download the app for iOS.
